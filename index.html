What are the advantages of Plumbed in Water Dispensers?

Plumbed in water coolers have been proven to be of a higher hygiene standard than bottled water. Many now are Direct Chill, this means the water dispensed is totally isolated from the surrounding atmosphere, affording even greater hygiene control with no storage reservoir.
Mains Fed (POU) Point of Use Water Coolers are more environmentally friendly, no more deliveries or running out of water at high usage times. removes bottle storage. Mains water coolers are an economic solution to supplying drinking water for your staff and family.
